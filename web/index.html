GD & Notice Portal

Our Project

The purpose of Notice & GD Portal is to provide students a platform for discussion over the current affairs and to be up to dated regarding the activities of CDC(Career Development Cell). This project is user-friendly. Individuals just have to fill up a form that specifies questions from all required fields such as name, branch, year, contact number, and so on. An authenticated account will be created for student after providing the necessary information. Paper work will be reduced as there would be no need to print the notice and forward to every single class. The second module is GD module constituting of two sides- admin side and user side. A topic related to current affairs or any general topic will be updated by administrator. Students will be provided with an option to put forward their views on the same. An additional benefit is that it provides a single platform to get the views of different students on a single topic and hence increases general awareness.
